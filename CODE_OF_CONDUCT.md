# Code Of Conduct

`.ontos Protocol` is an open-source project for people building clearer AI-era
project context.

We want the community to be direct, useful, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be specific when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent, but do not ignore real problems.
- Critique ideas and implementation details, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Help newcomers understand context.
- Prefer reproducible examples over vague objections.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Insults based on identity, background, language, or experience level.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Bad-faith contribution behavior, including spam and intentionally misleading
  reports.

## Reporting

For now, report conduct issues by opening a private security/contact channel once
the public repository is created. Until then, maintainers should handle issues
directly and document the decision privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, moderate, temporarily restrict, or ban participants who
harm the project community.

The goal is not bureaucracy. The goal is to keep the work clear and safe enough
for serious collaboration.
